Instructions

1. Press start

2. Press choose your screen and choose the screen you want to present (If you no longer want to present your screen, just press stop screen.)

3. Press recording to start recording the screen you choose, (If you no longer want to record your screen, just press stop recording.)

4. The video file is automatically saved when you finish recording the video.

5. IMPORTANT THINGS: You can only record your screen if you have already chosen which screen to record, obviously.

Ctrl or Cmd + W to close this window.
